About

My Journey Into Tech

From a young age, I was fascinated by how technology connects people. I remember using my first mobile phone and wondering, How do we communicate instantly? How does the internet work? How can one click bring the whole world to you? That curiosity pushed me into the world of tech, not just to understand it, but to build with it.

I started by learning Website Development, driven by a desire to understand how the online world works behind the scenes. As I grew, I discovered something bigger: Businesses — especially small and mid-sized ones in Nigeria were struggling because they lacked digital structures. They had great products… Great services… Great potential… But no online presence. No visibility. No digital strategy. No systems to convert customers.

I built Attendease A SaaS platform I built for schools across Nigeria that replaces manual attendance registers with QR card and facial recognition check-in. Parents are instantly notified by SMS when their child is absent, late, or leaves school — all automatically, with zero effort from staff.

Recently built Ruuvoo, a fintech platform I conceived and developed entirely from scratch — frontend to backend — .

I've also built AI-powered tools using the Claude API, adding a new layer of intelligence to the products I create.
